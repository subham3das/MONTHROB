FAQs

Your questions, answered.

Orders & Payments
How do I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number and a link. You can also track your order directly on our website through the "Track Order" link in the footer.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, Net Banking, and popular mobile wallets through our secure payment gateway. Cash on Delivery (COD) is also available for select pin codes.

Shipping & Delivery
How long will it take to receive my order?

Standard shipping usually takes 5-7 business days within India. Processing your order takes 1-3 business days before it is handed over to our courier partners.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we only ship within India. We do not provide international shipping services at this time.

Returns & Exchanges
What is your return policy?

We have a strict No Return and No Refund policy. Due to the exclusive nature of our drops, all sales are final.

Can I exchange a damaged item?

Exchanges are only provided for manufacturing defects or severe damage. You MUST provide a proper, continuous unboxing video of the brand-new parcel to be eligible for an exchange request. Contact support within 24 hours of delivery.

Products & Sizing
How do I know my size?

Every product page includes a detailed Size Chart. Most of our streetwear is designed for an oversized fit. If you prefer a more standard fit, we recommend sizing down.

Will you restock sold-out items?

Many of our collections are limited edition drops and will not be restocked once sold out. Follow us on Instagram to stay updated on new drops and potential restocks.
