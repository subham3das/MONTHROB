Shipping Policy

Last updated: May 3, 2026

Order Processing

We strive to get your order to you as quickly as possible. All orders are processed within 1-3 business days (excluding weekends and holidays) after receiving your order confirmation email.

You will receive another notification when your order has shipped.

Shipping Rates & Timeline

We offer standard shipping within India. All orders are delivered via our trusted courier partners.

Service Estimated Delivery Cost
Standard Shipping 5 - 7 Business Days Rs. 40 (Free over Rs. 5000)

Shipping Restrictions

International Shipping: We currently only offer shipping within India. We do not provide international shipping services at this time.

P.O. Boxes: Some carriers have limitations on shipping to P.O. Boxes. If your address is a P.O. Box, we may contact you for an alternative physical address to ensure successful delivery.

How do I check the status of my order?

When your order has shipped, you will receive an email notification from us which will include a tracking number you can use to check its status. Please allow 48 hours for the tracking information to become available.

Damages

Monthrob is not liable for any products damaged or lost during shipping. If you received your order damaged, please contact the shipment carrier to file a claim. Please save all packaging materials and damaged goods before filing a claim.
