Returns & Exchanges

Last updated: May 3, 2026

Strict No Return & No Refund Policy

Overview

At Monthrob, we maintain a very strict quality control process to ensure you receive the best products. Due to the limited nature of our drops and high demand, we do not offer returns or monetary refunds under any circumstances once an order has been delivered.

Exchange Only Exception

Exchanges are only permitted in the case of a manufacturing defect or if the product is received in severe damaged condition.

For an exchange to be considered:

  • The product must be in its original, brand-new condition.
  • All tags must be attached and untampered (no tears or removal).
  • The fabric must not have been washed, worn, or altered.

Mandatory Unboxing Video

To request an exchange for a damaged item, you MUST provide a full, proper, and continuous video of you opening the brand-new parcel.

Video Requirements:

  • The video must start BEFORE the parcel is opened.
  • The shipping label and tracking ID must be clearly visible in the video.
  • The video must show the entire unboxing process and the damaged area of the product in one single shot (no cuts or edits).

Requests without a valid unboxing video will be automatically rejected.

How to Request an Exchange

If you have met the requirements above, please contact our support team within 24 hours of delivery:

Email: contactmonthrob@gmail.com

Include your Order ID and attach the unboxing video. Our team will review the case and, if approved, will process the exchange for the same item (subject to availability).

Size & Preference

Please refer to our Size Chart carefully before ordering. We do not offer exchanges for size issues or changes in personal preference/mindset once the order is placed.
